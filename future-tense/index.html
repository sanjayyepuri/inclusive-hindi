a Non-Binary Voice in the Future (tense)

Factoring in a non-binary voice in the Future tense

In Hindi when talking about future plans or actions that will be performed in the future, one laregly uses the future tense. These are equivalent to the English, “I will come”, “I will see”, “I will conquer”.

To see how the future tense works with the non-binary voice proposed by the Making Hindi Grammar Inclusive project, check out the links below. Feel free to download and use both, the pdf and pages files:
